The *Tyrannosaurus* is the most **fierce** dinosaur that ever existed!

The T-Rex lived in the late [Cretaceous Period](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cretaceous).

## Features

- Big Teeth
- Small arms
- Thick tail
